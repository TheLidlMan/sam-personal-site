05 / operating principles

The website is not the work. It is the doorway.

The point is to make the right people understand the shape of the work quickly: practical, slightly obsessive, allergic to fake polish, and interested in systems that survive reality.

Make it inspectable.If a system hides everything interesting, it is probably hiding something broken.
Useful over theatrical.Good software changes what someone can do. It does not just wear a shiny landing page hat.
Privacy is a design constraint.Especially with personal data exports, automation, and AI-assisted workflows.
Keep the weirdness controlled.Memorable is good. Tacky is a tax. The difference matters.
06 / contact

Send the interesting problem.

If it involves web tools, automation, QA, security, events, data exports, or a small system nobody else wants to untangle, that is probably the right shape.
